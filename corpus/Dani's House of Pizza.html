Dani's house of pizza

 

Dani’s House of Pizza has been a Kew Gardens landmark since 1959. There’s a reason why we’ve been here for 50+ years: fantastic food, a friendly relaxed atmosphere, and reason-able prices are just a few of the reasons that Dani’s has proudly continued to be a pillar of the Kew Gardens community.

The renowned Dani’s House of Pizza was founded by Ramiz Dani in 1959. Ramiz was loved by the entire Kew Gardens community. He passed away in 2000 and the outpouring of sym-pathy was enormous. His son Sabri now continues the tradition of excellence that is Dani's House of Pizza.

Our special trademark sweet sauce is a family tradition which separates Dani's pizza slice from the rest. The pies are baked at the perfect temperature in our meticulously maintained and monitored pizza ovens. The result is a pizza-eating experience beyond comparison.
